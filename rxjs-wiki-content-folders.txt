Not directly - Claude can't play or transcribe audio/video. But there's a practical workaround depending on the source:

YouTube talks (most RxJS content)

YouTube generates transcripts automatically. Get one in seconds:

1. Open the video on YouTube
2. Click ".
3. Copy-paste the transcript into the chat > /wiki-ingest

The transcript quality is usually good enough for conference talks (Ben Lesh, Nicholas Jamieson, etc.).

CLI approach (any video file or YouTube URL)

If you have yt-dlp and OpenAI whisper installed:

# Download audio + transcribe
yt-dlp -x -- audio-format mp3 "https://youtube. com/watch?v =... " -o talk.mp3
whisper talk.mp3 -- output_format txt
# Then paste talk.txt into the chat /wiki-ingest

Or just the subtitle track (faster, no AI):

yt-dlp -- write-auto-sub -- skip-download -- sub-format vtt "https:// ... " -o transcript
# Convert vtt to plain text, then ingest
